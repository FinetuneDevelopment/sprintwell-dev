Warm courgette and toasted walnut salad

This tasty salad needs to be served as soon as it is ready to get full benefit of the flavour.

15 minutes 10 minutes

Ingredients

Salad:

  • ½ head of small Romaine lettuce
  • 55 g rocket
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped spring onions
  • 200 g courgette (two medium sized courgettes are approx. 200g)
  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 55 g chopped walnuts, toasted

Dressing:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp vegan mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ½ garlic clove, finely minced
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ⅛ tsp dried mustard powder

Nutritional information

Per serving:

  • Calories: 109 kcal
  • Carbs: 3 g
  • Fat: 11.2 g
  • Protein: 5 g

Cooking instructions

Step 1:

In a small bowl, whisk together all ingredients for the dressing. Set aside.

Step 2:

Trim and tear the salad. Place the Romaine, rocket and spring onions in a large bowl.

Step 3:

Cut the courgettes lengthwise and remove the seeds. Cut each of the courgette pieces into 2½cm across.

Step 4:

Heat olive oil in a frying pan over medium heat, do not allow to burn. Add courgettes to the pan, and season with salt and pepper. Sauté until just browning but still with a bite.

Step 5:

Toss the cooked courgettes in the lettuce mix and turn to combine.

Step 6:

Roast the nuts briefly in the same pan. Season with salt and pepper. Scatter over the salad, and drizzle with the dressing.
