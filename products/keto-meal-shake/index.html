We recommend water or plant milk but you could mix it with milk. Just remember to use whole milk for the higher fat content but watch out for amount of whole milk you use. One cup of whole milk contains 12g of carbohydrates.

The first phase is usually the adaptation phase, everyone is different for how long it takes to enter ketosis but usually we expect the first 10 days your body is getting use to relying on fat versus glucose or carbs for energy. To see the best results we really recommend you follow a keto diet for 3 months but we’d obviously hope you’d continue for longer!
