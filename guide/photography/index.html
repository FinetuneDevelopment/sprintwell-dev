Photography

General rules

  • Try and avoid direct address
  • Natural, domestic locations
  • Natural lighting
  • Colour
  • Avoid dramatic digital software filters, such as making the image appear to be a Polaroid photograph
  • People, rather than objects
  • Realism, rather than aspiration
  • Informal, rather than posed

Recipes

The key image of each recipe is reused on multiple pages and aspect ratios. To allow for the entire dish to appear, leave plenty of space around the edge of the plate. Try to keep this surrounding area low-contrast but with interesting textures.

Ingredients

Ingredients are sometimes used as backgrounds for images, which appear as a frame around the outside of the image. Take care to select ingredients which are compatible with a keto lifestyle. Below are a selection of such ingredients, sorted into colour.
