Foods

Ingredients are sometimes used as backgrounds for images, which appear as a frame around the outside of the image. Take care to select ingredients which are compatible with a keto lifestyle. Below are a selection of such ingredients, sorted into colour.

Photography of this kind should be relatively low contrast, with one dominant colour. The ingredients should be shot from a perpendicular angle, so as to flatten them into a more pattern-like image.
