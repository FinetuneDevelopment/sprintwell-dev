Tone of voice

Sprintwell is based around the idea of small communities of people coming together in order to lose weight using a keto diet. Because of this, Sprintwell content should encourage, inform and unite. Customers should feel the brand is approachable, knowledgeable and always has their best interests at heart.

Think of the brand personality as that of a kindly teacher who cares deeply for their pupils, but also wishes to maintain a professional detachment required, in order to maintain discipline.

Sprintwell is:

  • Informative
  • Occasionally technical
  • Gently encouraging
  • Calm
  • Measured (no hyperbolic claims)
  • Authoritative

Sprintwell is not:

  • Chummy
  • Laddish
  • Mocking
  • Witty
  • Arch
  • Informal

Ideally, Sprintwell should provide a platform where users create their own micro-communities where jokes and fun are part of the culture. The Sprintwell brand should remain at an emotional distance from this, while never disapproving of it.
