Why 10 days?

Old habits die hard. Rather than give your body a shock, we help you switch via a 10 day sprint. This allows your body time to adjust and we’re here to help you through those 10 days, every step of the way. We even recommend you take the weekend off the Sprint, because after all we’re just human and you’ll still lose weight!

Our Vision

Our vision is to help as many people as possible to transition to a keto lifestyle as possible. It’s not only incredibly healthy, we believe it’s absolutely life changing. No more diets, no more crash weight gains, no more feeling guilty or rubbish about ourselves.
